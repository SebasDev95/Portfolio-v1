Governlution

The dutch government is actively trying to digitalize its infrastructure. But as the government consists of many different departments and municipalities it is hard to map everyone’s digitalization. So a manual is created that explains eight steps to achieve the goal of digitalization. But since this manual is made up of a lot of dull information, it is not encouraging.

Therefore the government presented, in collaboration with Hogeschool Rotterdam, the case to create a digital tool that measures the adulthood from the digitalization process of every individual government instancy. The adulthood is a representation how far an instancy is with the process written in the manual.

The result is a Proof of Concept called: ‘Governlution’. An application for iOS and Android that tests the knowledge of the manual through the use of Augmented Reality.
