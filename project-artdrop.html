Artdrop.nl

Starting my second year of college, me and my fellow classmates were free to choose whatever project we wanted to do. After creating a team of five we decided to create a webshop, for artists to display and sell their artwork with a feature to customize products with artworks. For a good impression check out the demo below!

The result is a fully operational webshop. A registration service for customers and artists that can search, filter, comment, like, customize and buy products. Saving session information from the user in the database, for a customized experience when returning.

My contribution to the project was:

  • Design
  • Search Engine
  • Shopping Cart
  • Database
  • Filters


For more information take a look at the project's github link at the top!
